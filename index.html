Mothers deserve a break.
Spend your break with us:

  • Meet new moms and build new friendships
  • Get involved in interesting, relaxing and useful activities
  • Enjoy your hobbies
  • Discover new interests
  • Develop new skills
  • Find your own reason to join us

"When mother is happy, family is happy.
When family is happy, nation is happy."

Abdul Kalam

Mothers wellbeing club is funded by Parents Association of St Aidan's Primary School in Enniscorthy. It activates on a voluntary basis and undertakes a programme of activities that promotes the involvement of mothers.
